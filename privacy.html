Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 16, 2026

DataWorks LLC ("we", "us", "our") operates the SolarOpt mobile application ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Data That Stays on Your Device

All solar energy data — including production, consumption, battery, and appliance usage information — is stored exclusively on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or have access to any of this data. When you connect your Enphase account, the App communicates directly with the Enphase API from your device; we do not act as an intermediary and do not receive or store your Enphase credentials or energy data on any server.

Data We Collect

We collect only the minimal information that Google requires to manage your subscription through the Google Play Store. This may include:

  • Your Google Play purchase and subscription identifiers
  • Transaction history related to your SolarOpt subscription

This data is processed by Google and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We do not collect your name, email address, or any other personal information beyond what Google provides to manage your subscription.

Third-Party Services

The App connects to the following third-party services:

  • Enphase API: To retrieve your solar system data. This connection is made directly from your device. Your use of the Enphase API is subject to Enphase's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Billing: To manage your subscription. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties. We do not run analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.

Data Retention

Since your solar data is stored only on your device, you can delete it at any time by clearing the App's data or uninstalling it. Subscription records maintained by Google are subject to Google's data retention policies.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
